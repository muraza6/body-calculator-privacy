Body Calculator Pro - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 30, 2025

Overview

Body Calculator Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Health & Fitness Data: Age, gender, height, weight, activity level, and fitness goals that you voluntarily provide for calculations
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality and crash reporting

What We DON'T Collect

  • We do NOT collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do NOT store your health data on external servers
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties
  • We do NOT track your location

How We Use Information

Local Processing Only

  • All calculations are performed locally on your device
  • Your health data is stored only on your device and never transmitted to our servers
  • Data is used solely to provide you with accurate body composition and nutrition calculations

App Improvement

  • Anonymous crash reports and app performance data may be collected to improve the app experience
  • No personal or health information is included in these reports

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All your personal health data is stored locally on your device using secure Android storage mechanisms
  • Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can clear data manually through your device's app settings

No Cloud Storage

  • We do not store any of your personal information on cloud servers or external databases
  • Your privacy is protected by keeping all sensitive data on your device only

Third-Party Services

Analytics (Optional)

  • We may use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand app usage patterns
  • This data is anonymized and does not include personal health information
  • You can opt out through your device's advertising settings
  • Body Calculator Pro does not display advertisements
  • We do not share data with advertising networks

    • Your Rights and Controls

    Data Control

    • Access: All your data is visible within the app interface
    • Modification: You can update your information at any time
    • Deletion: Uninstall the app to permanently delete all data
    • Portability: Your data exists only on your device

    Opt-Out Options

    • Disable analytics through your device settings
    • Use airplane mode for completely offline calculations

    Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

    Age Requirements

    Minimum Age: Body Calculator Pro is intended for users 13 years of age and older. This app is NOT designed for or directed to children under 13 years of age.

    No Data Collection from Children Under 13

    • We do NOT knowingly collect, store, use, or disclose any personal information from children under 13 years of age
    • We do NOT knowingly allow children under 13 to create accounts or input personal data
    • Our app does not contain features specifically designed to attract children under 13

    Parental Responsibilities

    If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided personal information to our app:

    • Contact us immediately at support@bodycalculatorpro.com
    • We will promptly delete any such information from our systems
    • We recommend parental supervision for any health and fitness app usage by minors

    Medical Guidance for Minors

    • Health and fitness calculations may not be appropriate for children under 13
    • We strongly recommend consulting healthcare professionals for children's nutrition and fitness guidance
    • Parents should supervise and approve any health-related app usage by children aged 13-17

    COPPA Compliance Statement

    This application fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and does not collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Any inadvertent collection of such information will be immediately deleted upon discovery.
